How to Apply

  2. How to Apply

{{'Create a new account or log in using your mobile number and password' | translate }}

  • Click Registration link at the portal, if you are new user.
  • Existing users can click on the login button.

{{'View the schemes and services information along with guideline of the department for applying application' | translate }}

  • Click Scheme link at the portal to view the schemes details.
  • Click Service link at the portal to view the services details.

{{'Get started or continue work on your application today' | translate }}

  • Provide all necessary Farmer, Schemes, Upload Documents, and bank information.
  • Click on the “Save as draft” button to draft the application or click on “Next” button to save and proceed with the application form.
  • Click on the confirm button for final submission of the application.
  • Click on the modify button to modify the draft application, if required.

{{'Know the application current status' | translate }}

  • Click on the “Track Status” link on the menu.
  • Give your application ID or Mobile Number to get the application status.

{{'Update your information and keep track of already completed applications' | translate }}

  • Click Change Password link to change the password.
  • Click Forgot Password link to reset the password.
